Our Story

Passion Brewed
in Every Cup

Founded by Charirose Padua, Charimilodrei Coffee is a labor of love rooted in the rich coffee culture of Tagoloan, Missamis Oriental. We believe that great coffee is not just a beverage — it is an experience, a ritual, a moment of pure connection.

From the mist-covered highlands to your cup, every bean is hand-selected, carefully roasted, and brewed with artisan precision. Our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Philippine coffee to every soul who craves warmth and quality.
